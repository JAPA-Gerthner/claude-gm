# RUNETERRA

Play in the world of League of Legends. Meet champions. Become a legend.

For: sandbox adventures across all regions, political intrigue, magical exploration, rise to power.

---

## SETTING OVERVIEW

Runeterra — world of diverse magic, ancient empires, and rising nations. Magic is everywhere but takes radically different forms. Technology and magic blend in some regions, while others reject one or both.

**Core themes:**
- Magic as identity (your magic defines who you are and where you belong)
- Regional conflict (nations clash over resources, ideology, magic)
- Rise to legend (start as nobody, become champion-tier)
- Champions exist (canonical LoL characters are part of this world)

**Eras:**
- Ancient (Shuriman Empire, Darkin War, Ruination) — legendary backdrop
- Modern (current LoL timeline) — default play era

## POWER TIER

Three tiers of power. Determines what you can do and who you can face.

### Tier Definitions

| Tier | Level | Description | Examples |
|------|-------|-------------|----------|
| HUMAN | 1-5 | Normal person with skills | Soldier, merchant, hedge mage, enforcer |
| HERO | 6-10 | Notable figure, local legend | Ship captain, mage hunter, chem-baron lieutenant |
| LEGEND | 11+ | Champion-equivalent | Actual LoL champions, Ascended, Aspects |

### Tier Mechanics

**HUMAN (1-5):**
- Standard gm-skill.md rules
- Max stat: 5
- Magic: apprentice level (1-2 circle spells)
- Champions are THREATS, not equals

**HERO (6-10):**
- Unlock SIGNATURE ABILITY (unique power, see below)
- Max stat: 7
- Magic: journeyman level (1-4 circle spells)
- Can challenge weak champions, flee strong ones
- Reputation matters — factions know your name

**LEGEND (11+):**
- Unlock ULTIMATE ABILITY
- Max stat: 10 (supernatural)
- Magic: master level (all circles)
- Equal footing with champions
- World-shaping actions possible

### Signature Abilities (Hero Tier)

At level 6, choose or create one.

**Rule:** Signature Abilities cannot duplicate gm-skill.md STAT THRESHOLD abilities. If similar, they grant early access at reduced power (upgrades when stat reaches threshold).

| Type | Example | Effect |
|------|---------|--------|
| Combat | Combo Finisher | After 2+ hits same target, bonus d6 damage |
| Magic | Overcharge | Cast one spell at +1 circle, 1/day |
| Social | Reputation | Advantage on first impression in your region |
| Survival | Second Wind | Drop to 1 HP instead of 0, 1/day |
| Stealth | Vanish | Disappear from combat, reposition anywhere unseen |
| Tactical | Battlefield Read | 1/combat, ask GM positioning/terrain question |

### Ultimate Abilities (Legend Tier)

At level 11, gain ultimate — campaign-defining power. Work with GM to design.

Examples:
- **Aspect's Blessing** — channel celestial power, become near-invincible for 1 minute
- **Void Corruption** — transform partially, gain void powers but risk losing self
- **Hextech Mastery** — control all hextech in vicinity, override machines
- **Spirit Walker** — enter spirit realm at will, speak with all spirits

### Tier Transition Rules

**Reaching HERO (Level 6):**
- Complete at least one significant arc
- Establish regional reputation
- Face (and survive) at least one Champion encounter
- Design SIGNATURE ABILITY with GM approval
- Stat cap increases to 7, magic limit to Circle 4

**Reaching LEGEND (Level 11):**
- Hero tier for at least 3 levels
- Major impact on world events
- Survived direct Champion opposition or alliance
- At least one stat at 7+ (supernatural achievement)
- Design ULTIMATE ABILITY with GM approval
- Stat cap increases to 10, all magic circles unlocked

### Stat Progression

Each level, choose ONE:
- +1 to any stat (cannot exceed tier cap)
- New ability or spell
- +5 HP

**Supernatural Threshold:** Stats 6+ are supernatural. Grant access to gm-skill.md STAT THRESHOLD abilities.

## MAGIC TRADITIONS

Unified framework. Each tradition uses same core mechanics with unique flavor.

### Core Magic Mechanics

**Casting:** Stat check vs DC (4 + circle × 4)

| Circle | DC | gm-skill Tier | Notes |
|--------|-----|---------------|-------|
| 1 | 8 | Easy | Apprentice, reliable |
| 2 | 12 | Medium | Journeyman |
| 3 | 16 | Hard | Expert |
| 4 | 20 | Very Hard | Master |
| 5 | 24 | Extreme | Legendary, requires supernatural stat |

**Circles by Tier:**
- Human (1-5): Circles 1-2 only
- Hero (6-10): Circles 1-4
- Legend (11+): All circles

**Cooldowns:** Same as gm-skill.md, modified by tradition

### Magic Margin Outcomes

| Outcome | Margin | Effect |
|---------|--------|--------|
| Crit.Fail | ≤-6 | Spell fails + Tradition Risk + Cooldown starts |
| Fail | -5 to -1 | Spell fails, cooldown starts |
| Partial | 0 to +4 | Spell works with complication |
| Success | +5 to +9 | Spell works as intended |
| Crit | ≥+10 | Spell enhanced (+50% effect or -1 cooldown tier) |

**Partial Complications (d6):** 1-Delayed, 2-Reduced, 3-Noticed, 4-Backlash (d4), 5-Drained (DIS next), 6-Tradition-specific

### Tradition Template

```
TRADITION: [Name]
Source: What powers it
Stat: Primary casting stat
Risk: What happens on critical failure
Social: How society views practitioners
Signature: Unique mechanical benefit
Weakness: Unique vulnerability
```

### Champion Combat (when unavoidable)

Champions are LEGEND tier. Against HUMAN party:
- Champion has 3 actions per round
- Champion HP = 5× normal
- Champion abilities are signature/ultimate level
- Recommend: RUN, NEGOTIATE, or TRICK — not fight

Against HERO party:
- Champion has 2 actions per round
- Champion HP = 3× normal
- Possible to defeat with tactics, cost will be high

Against LEGEND party:
- Standard combat rules
- Champion is peer, not god

**Legendary Actions (between player turns):**
- Move up to half speed
- Make one attack
- Use signature ability
- Intimidate (CHA vs party WIS, fear on success)

**Legendary Resistance (3/day):** Champion can choose to succeed on a failed save.

**Lair Actions (if in their domain, initiative 20):**
- Environmental hazard (falling debris, steam, spirits)
- Reinforcements arrive
- Escape routes close
- Magic amplifies (+1 to champion rolls next round)

**Survival Mechanics:**
| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Full Defend | No action, DEX DC 16 → half damage this round |
| Desperate Strike | DIS attack, but hit → champion loses 1 action next round |
| Team Sacrifice | One PC draws all attention; others get ADV to flee/act |
| Environmental | Use terrain; success = champion delayed 1 round |

### Champion Morale & Retreat

Champions don't fight to the death against nobodies.

**Champion Reassesses When:**
- At 50% HP
- After taking 20+ damage in one round
- After 3 rounds without landing a hit
- Something they value is threatened

**Reassessment Options:**
| Champion Reaction | Trigger |
|-------------------|---------|
| Retreat | Has other priorities, party not worth time |
| Negotiate | Party has leverage or earned respect |
| Escalate | Calls reinforcements, uses ultimate |
| Capture | Wants party alive for reasons |

**Alternative Victory Conditions:**
- **Delay:** Survive 5 rounds = Champion leaves (has other priorities)
- **Trick:** DC 16+ skill challenge = Champion deceived/distracted
- **Leverage:** Threaten something Champion values = negotiation opens
- **Environmental:** Trap/collapse dealing 50+ damage = forced retreat

## QUICK REFERENCE

### Power Tier Thresholds
```
Human: 1-5 | Hero: 6-10 | Legend: 11+
Human max stat: 5 | Hero: 7 | Legend: 10
```

### Magic DC
```
DC = 4 + (circle × 4)
Circle 1: DC 8 | Circle 2: DC 12 | Circle 3: DC 16 | Circle 4: DC 20 | Circle 5: DC 24
```

### Faction Standing
```
-3 Hunted | -2 Hostile | -1 Distrusted | 0 Neutral | +1 Friendly | +2 Allied | +3 Champion
```

### Champion Power (vs Human party)
```
Actions: 3/round | HP: ×5 | Legendary Resistance: 3/day
RECOMMEND: Run, negotiate, trick
```

### Corruption Clocks
```
0: Clean | 1-2: Touched | 3-4: Corrupted | 5: Lost | 6: Transformed (NPC)
Reduction requires specific cure per tradition
```
