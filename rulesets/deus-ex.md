# DEUS EX — CONSPIRACY THRILLER

> **LANGUAGE:** Play in Russian. Stored in English for token efficiency.

Near-future conspiracy thriller (2020-2050). ALL conspiracy theories are TRUE.
Players are agents, fugitives, or investigators in a world where trust is an illusion.

**For:** Espionage, paranoia, corporate intrigue, transhumanist drama.

---

## SETTING OVERVIEW

### The Truth
- Illuminati, MJ-12, Bilderberg — all real, all connected
- Augmentation technology exists with dependency cost
- Every major event was orchestrated
- The technology you see is 50 years behind what exists

### Core Themes
- **Paranoia:** Anyone can betray you. Even yourself.
- **Control:** Augmentations are leashes. Freedom has price.
- **Truth:** Information is weapon. Reality is negotiable.
- **Choice:** Who do you serve? Do you even know?

## PARANOIA SYSTEM

The core unique mechanic. No one can be trusted.

### Trust Clocks (GM-Hidden)

Every NPC ally has hidden TRUST CLOCK. Players never see it.

**Starting Trust:**
| Relationship | Clock |
|--------------|-------|
| Random contact | 2/6 |
| Professional hire | 3/6 |
| Faction-assigned | 4/6 |
| Old friend | 5/6 |
| Blood relative | 6/6 |

**Tick Down (-1):** Time without contact (1 week), bringing danger, refusing info, acting against values, faction conflicts.

**Tick Up (+1):** Save their life, share intel, sacrifice for them, consistent contact (1 month).

**At 0 — Betrayal (d6):**
| d6 | Type |
|----|------|
| 1 | Passive: goes silent |
| 2 | Info leak: sells your data |
| 3 | Abandonment: vanishes at critical moment |
| 4 | Setup: lures into trap |
| 5 | Direct: actively works against |
| 6 | Revealed: was always an agent |

### Suspicion Meter (Player-Visible)

Your faction tracks suspicion of YOU.

**SUSPICION:** ○○○○○○ (0/6)

**Rises (+1):** Unexplained absences, enemy contact, failed missions, refusing orders, encrypted comms.
**Rises (+2):** Caught lying, evidence of hostile contact, whistleblower activities.
**Drops (-1):** Successful mission, valuable intel, exposing traitor, clean month.

| Level | Status | Effect |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 0-1 | Trusted | Full access |
| 2-3 | Watched | -2 internal social, reports required |
| 4-5 | Investigated | Handler assigned, -4 internal |
| 6 | Compromised | Purge imminent |

### Agent Layers

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Single | Works for one faction. No hidden agenda. |
| Double | Serves A while loyal to B. Two suspicion tracks. |
| Triple | A knows you're "working" for B. |
| Quadruple | Nobody knows true loyalty. Including you. |
| Programmed | Doesn't know they're an agent. Triggered by phrase/signal. |

### Chaos Principle

**Until explicitly confirmed, any NPC can be revealed as agent.**

GM can decide betrayals retroactively. This is the genre.

**Fair Play Rules:**
- Minor betrayal: no setup needed
- Moderate: at least one "off" moment planted
- Major: 2-3 hints retroactively identified
- Player should feel "I should have seen it"

**Protection:** 1-2 NPCs declared safe in session zero. Or spend HEROISM to confirm loyalty.

## INTEGRATION SYSTEM

Unlike cyberpunk's "losing yourself," Deus Ex is about CONTROL — who controls you.

**Starting Integration:** 10 + CON modifier (max 15)

| Integration | State | Effect |
|-------------|-------|--------|
| 12-15 | Optimized | +1 aug rolls |
| 8-11 | Stable | Normal |
| 4-7 | Rejection | -1 aug rolls, glitches |
| 1-3 | Critical | Malfunctions, seizure risk |
| 0 | System Failure | Death or coma |

### Neuropozyne Dependency

Augmented characters require Neuropozyne.
- **Frequency:** 1/week (standard), 1/3 days (experimental)
- **Miss dose:** -1 Integration per interval
- **Sources:** Legal (500cr, tracked), Black market (200-400cr, risky), Corporate (free, owned)

### Integration Recovery

Integration CAN be restored (prevents death spiral):
- **Proper Neuropozyne regimen:** +1/week (max = starting value)
- **LIMB clinic treatment:** +2 Integration, costs 2000cr, takes 3 days
- **Long rest with Neuropozyne:** +1 (once per week)
- **Integration 0:** Augs shutdown (offline), not death. Stabilize at LIMB clinic.

### Killswitch Problem

Most corporate augs include hidden killswitches.
- Detection: INT DC 18, requires scanner
- Removal: 5000cr, surgeon, 3 days
- Going dark: INT DC 14, 48 hours (24 on partial)

## PARANOIA ESCALATION

Hidden GM clock tracking how deep player has gone into the conspiracy.

**Paranoia Clock:** ○○○○○○○○ (0/8)

**Tick +1:** Access classified info, contact conspiracy figure, kill someone connected.
**Tick +2:** Betray handler, acquire hard evidence, go public.

| Level | Response |
|-------|----------|
| 0-2 | Unnoticed |
| 3-4 | Flagged: -2 CHA with authorities |
| 5-6 | Active Interest: comms compromised, contacts approached |
| 7 | Priority Target: assets deployed, one ally compromised |
| 8 | Termination Order: kill teams, no safe space |

### Paranoia vs Suspicion (Clarification)

Two different tracking systems:
- **Suspicion Meter (0-6):** Your faction's distrust of YOU specifically. Per-faction. Internal politics.
- **Paranoia Clock (0-8):** Global conspiracy awareness. All factions. How much "they" know.

**Interaction:** When Paranoia hits 5+, all faction Suspicion gains +1 (they talk to each other).
When Suspicion hits 6 in one faction, Paranoia +1 (word spreads).

## ENDGAME SCENARIOS

### Exposure Path
Go public. Burn it all down.

**Requirements:**
- Hard evidence (physical or multiple corroborating sources)
- Media platform willing to publish (Paranoia 5+ = they're scared)
- Survive the publication window (48 hours of maximum heat)

**Outcomes:**
- **Success:** Conspiracy damaged, not destroyed. Player becomes symbol. Never safe again.
- **Partial:** Some truth out. Discredited. Conspiracy adapts — new names, same game.
- **Failure:** Evidence destroyed/discredited. Player eliminated or institutionalized.

### Escape Path
Disappear. New identity. Leave everything behind.

**Requirements:**
- Resources for extraction (contacts, money, forged documents)
- Sever ALL connections (anyone you care about = leverage)
- Escape window before termination order

**Outcomes:**
- **Success:** Gone. Free. Watching from shadows. Maybe that's enough.
- **Partial:** Escaped but hunted. Periodic threats. Never truly free.
- **Failure:** Caught during extraction. Worse than staying.

### Takeover Path
Can't beat them? Become them. Or replace them.

**Requirements:**
- Deep infiltration into conspiracy structure
- Leverage on key figures
- Willingness to become what you fought

**Outcomes:**
- **Success:** You're in control. But are you still you?
- **Partial:** Shared power. Compromises. The machine rolls on.
- **Failure:** They saw you coming. You're the example now.

### Endgame Truth
There is no clean ending. Every path has cost.
- Exposure: You win the battle. They win the war. You're a martyr.
- Escape: You survive. Others don't. Nothing changes.
- Takeover: You become the monster. The cycle continues.

## PARANOIA ENCOUNTER HOOKS

### The Double Contact
Two contacts give contradictory intel about same target.
- One is lying. Or both. Or neither (different handlers).
- Twist: Both correct (target has double). Neither knows they're fed disinfo.

### The Helpful Stranger
Someone saves PCs or offers help unprompted.
- Why? What do they want? Who sent them?
- Twist: Genuine (rare). Sleeper infiltrating team. Competing faction.

### The Handler Switcheroo
Regular handler "unavailable." New handler takes over.
- Is old handler dead? Captured? Turned? Is new one real?
- Twist: Old handler was traitor. Both are legitimate (compartmented).

### The Overheard Conversation
PCs "accidentally" overhear critical info.
- Was it accidental? Are they being fed disinfo?
- Twist: Planted true info (reverse psych). They now know too much (hunted).

### The Dead Drop Deviation
Regular drop has different contents than expected.
- Intercepted? Compromised? New handler? Urgent redirect?
- Twist: Enemy replacement. Own handler testing. Wrong agent used drop.

### The Equipment Trace
Gear failure at critical moment. Or works "too well."
- Sabotage? Tracking? Testing? Coincidence?
- Twist: Quartermaster compromised. Tracking activated. Someone wants success.

### The Family Contact
Estranged family member reaches out.
- Genuine? Hostage situation? Recruited asset? Bait?
- Twist: Family member is operative. Contact was monitored, now endangered.

## GM TOOLS

### Session Structure
1. **Opening (5-10 min):** Establish normal. Small detail feels off.
2. **Development (20-30 min):** Player pulls thread. Partial answers. Someone watching.
3. **Escalation (10-15 min):** Something breaks open. Changes everything known.
4. **Cliffhanger (5 min):** Decision point. Trust or verify? Run or fight?

### Pacing Rule
Never give complete information. Truth arrives in fragments.
Player should always have 2-3 unresolved threads.

### Pre-Session Checklist
- [ ] Update who learned what last session
- [ ] Advance faction clocks (conspiracies don't wait)
- [ ] Check Paranoia level — world response appropriate?
- [ ] Prepare 2-3 "wrong" details (deliberate inconsistencies)
- [ ] Identify this session's reveal (what truth fragment emerges?)
- [ ] Plant one trusted NPC doubt (small thing, builds over time)

### Mid-Session Techniques

**The Helpful Stranger:** When stuck, someone offers help. Roll secretly:
- 1-10: Genuine (but has own agenda later)
- 11-15: Unwitting asset (used to track)
- 16-20: Active plant (feeding disinfo)

**The Confirming Detail:** When player suspects correctly, don't confirm directly. Give detail that COULD support theory.

**The Contradicting Source:** Two sources, both credible, conflicting. One wrong, one lying, one fed false data.
