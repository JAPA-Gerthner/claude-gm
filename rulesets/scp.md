# SCP FOUNDATION — BUREAUCRATIC HORROR

> **LANGUAGE:** Play in Russian. Stored in English for token efficiency.

Modern procedural horror. Players are Foundation personnel: D-Class, researchers, MTF operatives.
Survive anomalies, bureaucracy, and internal politics. The paperwork protects you from the horror.

**For:** Containment operations, internal investigations, survival horror, conspiracy.

---

## SETTING OVERVIEW

### The Foundation
- Secure. Contain. Protect.
- Global organization predating most governments
- Contains anomalies threatening consensus reality
- Operates through bureaucracy, compartmentalization, secrecy
- Personnel are expendable; containment is eternal

### Core Themes
- **Procedural Horror:** Follow protocol or die. Protocol might still kill you.
- **Expendability:** "D-Class" isn't a rank — it's a reminder.
- **Compartmentalization:** What you don't know can't compromise you.
- **Institutional Decay:** The Foundation is slowly losing.

### The Truth
- Multiple contradictory origin stories (all partially true)
- The Administrator exists (probably)
- O5 Council rules through consensus (usually)
- Ethics Committee has teeth (sometimes)
- Nobody knows the full picture (especially you)

## CORE MECHANICS: USEFULNESS & LIABILITY

The Foundation measures everything. Including you.

### USEFULNESS (0-10)
Your value to the Foundation. Why they keep you alive.

**USEFULNESS:** ●●●●●○○○○○ (5/10)

| Level | Status | Effect |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 0 | Expendable | D-Class treatment, terminal assignments |
| 1-3 | Marginal | Basic assignments, watched |
| 4-6 | Valued | Resources, some autonomy, protection |
| 7-9 | Essential | Priority resources, backup teams, extraction protocols |
| 10 | Irreplaceable | O5 attention, personal protection details |

**Gain (+1):**
- Successful containment/recovery
- Valuable research contribution
- Surviving what shouldn't survive
- Information critical to operations
- Exceptional performance review

**Lose (-1):**
- Mission failure (partial)
- Resource waste
- 3 months without notable contribution
- Someone else does your job better

### Usefulness & Liability: Timing

Award at END OF SESSION or END OF OPERATION, not per-action.

| Outcome | U | L | Notes |
|---------|---|---|-------|
| Success (clean) | +1 | +0 | — |
| Partial | +0 | +0 | — |
| Failure | -1 | +0 | — |
| Protocol violation | +0 | +1 | Once per op, not per action |
| Failed psych eval | +0 | +1 | Roleplay-level |
| Impressed superior | +1 | +0 | Roleplay-level |
| Anomalous exposure | +0 | +1 | Roleplay-level |

Major violations (breach caused, insubordination) stack separately.

### LIABILITY (0-10)
How much trouble you represent. Why they might terminate you.

**LIABILITY:** ●●●○○○○○○○ (3/10)

| Level | Status | Effect |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 0-2 | Clean | Normal operations |

## PROCESSING CLOCK

When LIABILITY exceeds USEFULNESS, Processing begins.

**PROCESSING:** ○○○○ (0/4)

**Tick Rate (based on Liability - Usefulness):**
| Excess | Weekly Tick | Notes |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| 0-1 | +0 | Clock stable, closing the gap |
| 2 | +1 | Standard processing rate |
| 3+ | +1 | Plus may add +1 on failed psych eval |

**Additional Ticks:**
- Failed psychological evaluation: +1 (if excess 3+, already counted in weekly)
- Another incident while under review: +1
- Major breach while under review: +2
- Evidence of compromised loyalty: +2

**At 4/4 — Processing Complete (d6):**
| d6 | Outcome |
|----|---------|
| 1 | Class-A Amnestics: Reset to civilian, monitored |
| 2 | Class-B Amnestics: Stay in Foundation, demoted |
| 3 | D-Class Reassignment: Orange jumpsuit |
| 4 | Indefinite Containment: You're an SCP now |
| 5 | Termination: Clean |
| 6 | Termination: Messy (body never found) |

**Prevention:**
- Usefulness spike (critical success) = -2 Processing
- Patron intervention = pause clock (costs favor)
- Ethics Committee appeal = DC 18 CHA + evidence
- Closing the gap: reduce excess below 2 to stabilize

Information is compartmentalized. Higher clearance = more truth = more danger.

| Level | Title | Access | Liability |
|-------|-------|--------|-----------|
| 0 | D-Class | Test chamber only | +0 baseline |
| 1 | Personnel | Public SCPs, basic protocols | +0 |
| 2 | Researcher | Full SCP files, Site data | +1 accumulated |
| 3 | Senior | Cross-reference, incident reports | +2 accumulated |
| 4 | Director | O5 communications, black sites | +3 accumulated |
| 5 | O5 | Everything. Maybe. | ??? |

**Clearance determines:**
- What you can read (files, reports, communications)
- Where you can go (zones, sites, areas)
- Who you can contact (personnel, O5, Ethics)
- What equipment you can requisition

**Accumulated Liability:** Higher clearance = more secrets = more ways to become a liability. Permanent.

## SAMPLE ANOMALIES

### SCP-173 (The Sculpture)
- **Class:** Euclid | **Disruption:** Vlam | **Risk:** Critical
- **Effect:** Locomotion occurs only when line of sight is broken. Documented lethality via cervical fracture.
- **Mechanic:** If all observers blink/look away (WIS DC 12 each round to maintain observation), subject moves. CON DC 18 or instantaneous termination.
- **Containment:** Minimum 3 personnel for any interaction. Eye contact rotation mandatory. Regular cleaning required (subject produces biological waste of unknown composition).

### SCP-914 (The Clockworks)
- **Class:** Safe | **Disruption:** Dark | **Risk:** Warning
- **Effect:** Input-output refinement device. Setting determines transformation parameters.
- **Settings:** Rough | Coarse | 1:1 | Fine | Very Fine
- **Mechanic:** Roll d20 for output quality. Very Fine setting: 1-10 hazardous output, 11-15 unexpected transformation, 16-20 improved iteration.
- **Protocols:** Level 3 authorization required. Safety protocols mandatory. D-Class personnel for retrieval.

### SCP-096 (The Shy Guy)
- **Class:** Euclid | **Disruption:** Ekhi | **Risk:** Critical
- **Effect:** Viewing facial features triggers pursuit response. Target acquisition is absolute.
- **Mechanic:** Face viewed = target marked. Subject will reach target regardless of distance or obstacles. Engagement not recommended.
- **Containment:** No visual documentation exists. Containment cell lacks monitoring equipment. Steel enclosure, no windows.
- **Note:** Targeting pattern analysis suggests possible sapience. Permanent containment remains theoretical.

### SCP-999 (The Tickle Monster)
- **Class:** Safe | **Disruption:** Dark | **Risk:** Notice
- **Effect:** Physical contact induces pronounced euphoric response. Documented therapeutic applications.
- **Mechanic:** Contact = +2 all social checks for 24h, remove fear/despair conditions.
- **Containment:** Standard Safe-class protocols. Subject is cooperative.
- **Note:** Anomaly displays consistent benevolent behavior. Reclassification to Thaumiel under review.

## COMBAT & EQUIPMENT

### Combat vs Anomalies
**Most anomalies cannot be defeated through combat.**

Options when encountering hostile anomaly:
1. **Containment:** Lure to trap, seal, neutralize
2. **Avoidance:** Don't engage, maintain distance
3. **Delay:** Buy time for backup/extraction
4. **Sacrifice:** Someone stays behind

### Anomaly Engagement

When attempting to counter an anomaly:

**1. Identify Hazard** (INT/WIS DC 12): Fail = -2 to counter. Success = +2 to counter.

**2. Propose Counter-Tactic:** Player describes approach, GM evaluates.

**3. Roll Against Resistance:**
| Class | DC |
|-------|-----|
| Safe | 12 |
| Euclid | 14 |
| Keter | 16 |
| Apollyon | 20+ |

**4. Resolve:** Partial+ = tactic works temporarily. Fail = escalation (+2 DC next attempt). Crit Fail = cascade.

**Note:** "Counter" = suppress/evade/neutralize temporarily. Permanent containment requires protocols.

### Escalation Risk
Each round of combat vs anomaly: Roll d6

| d6 | Escalation |
|----|------------|
| 1 | Anomaly retreats (temporary) |
| 2-3 | Stalemate continues |
| 4-5 | Situation worsens (+2 DC all checks) |
| 6 | Cascade event (additional threat) |

| Hume | State | Effect |

| 0 | Neutral | Self-interest only |

**The Talk:** When colleague's trust reaches critical decision:
1. Denial: "Everything's fine" (buying time)

## STARTER SCENARIO: ORIENTATION

**Setup:** New D-Class transferred to Site-19. First week.
**Handler:** Dr. Ellis Chen (Clearance 3, distant but fair)
**Assignment:** SCP-914 testing — input objects, document outputs, survive.

**Read to Players:** "SCP-914 testing rotation. Follow instructions. Do not touch the dial. Do not enter output booth until cleared. Questions are above your clearance."

**The Test:** Input wristwatch. Setting: Fine. **Complication (d6):**

| d6 | Complication |
|----|--------------|
| 1 | Output is cognitohazardous |
| 2 | Second object appears (wasn't in input) |
| 3 | Watch outputs as biological tissue |
| 4 | Power fluctuation, dial moved on its own |
| 5 | Previous subject (thought terminated) in booth |
| 6 | Reflection in output isn't you |

**NPCs:** Chen (intercom, testing you). Site Director (walks through, says nothing). Fellow D-Class (rumors, may be informant).

**Resolution:** Protocol = +1U. Handle complication = +1U. Violation = +1L, WITNESS +1. Panic = +2L, Processing.

### Site-Based
Stationed at one Site. Deep politics, recurring NPCs.
- **Start:** Established position, existing relationships, local crisis
- **Arc:** Navigate politics, handle breaches, protect allies
- **Stakes:** Site Stability clock threatens everything

### Field Team
MTF or mobile researchers. Operations across locations.
- **Start:** Team assignment, handler contact, first mission
- **Arc:** Complete operations, bond with team, discover pattern
- **Stakes:** Casualty rate, team cohesion, institutional trust

### Internal Investigation
Something's wrong inside. You're finding out what.
- **Start:** Evidence of problem, mandate to investigate, enemies forming
- **Arc:** Gather evidence, avoid compromised personnel, decide what to do
- **Stakes:** Becoming the liability, truth worse than expected

### Whistleblower
You know too much. You're getting out.
- **Start:** Critical information, compromised position, exit plan
- **Arc:** Survive extraction, evade pursuit, decide destination
- **Stakes:** Everything and everyone you trusted

## END STATES

### Death
- Anomaly exposure (immediate)
- Termination order (processed)
- Field casualty (operational)
- Friendly fire (suspicious)
- Suicide (documented)

### Amnestics
- Class A-C: Continue working (demoted/reset)
- Class W: Targeted concept removal (continue with gaps)
- Class Z: New identity (effectively new character)

### D-Class Reassignment
- Processing complete
- Demotion extreme
- Useful enough to live, not enough to value

### Defection
- GOI recruitment (Chaos Insurgency, Serpent's Hand)
- Independent escape (always hunted)
- "Death" while actually extracted

### Ascension
- O5 Council membership (rare, possibly fatal)
- Ethics Committee position
- Site Director (success = target)
