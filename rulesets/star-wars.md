# STAR WARS

A galaxy far, far away. Rebels, smugglers, Jedi, Sith. For: underdog rebellion, scoundrel capers, Force drama, galactic adventure.

> **LANGUAGE:** Preferred session language is **Russian**. This ruleset is stored in English for token efficiency only.

---

## SETTING OVERVIEW

**Eras:** Old Republic | Clone Wars | Imperial | New Republic | Sequel
**Tone:** Space opera with grit. Hope against tyranny, moral complexity, found family.
**Core themes:** Light vs Dark (not good vs evil), freedom vs order, legacy, redemption.

Technology is stagnant across eras — what changes is who holds power and how rare the Force is.

**Key Lore:**
- The Force binds all living things. Some can feel and manipulate it.
- Jedi serve peace through serenity. Sith seek power through passion.
- The Rule of Two: After 1000 BBY, only two Sith exist — master and apprentice.
- Hyperspace allows faster-than-light travel. Requires calculations or risk death.
- Droids are common. Some develop personalities. Legal status varies.
- Aliens face discrimination in Empire. The Rebellion is diverse by necessity.

## CULTURAL TRADITIONS

### The Mandalorian Way (Resol'nare)

Six Acts:
1. Wear armor
2. Speak Mando'a
3. Defend self and family
4. Raise children as Mandalorian
5. Contribute to clan welfare
6. Rally to Mand'alor when called

**Breaking the Way:**
| Violation | Consequence |
|-----------|-------------|
| Helmet removed* | -2 Honor, shame |
| Abandoning foundling | -3 Honor, exile risk |
| Selling beskar | -2 Honor |
| Fighting for Empire | Dar'manda (exile) |

*Helmet rule applies to Children of the Watch. Mainstream Mandalorians (Bo-Katan, Sabine) have no such restriction.

### Wookiee Life Debt

When a Wookiee's life is saved:
- Must offer life debt to savior
- Serves until debt repaid (subjective)
- Breaking debt = permanent dishonor
- Death in service fulfills debt

### Jedi Code

*There is no emotion, there is peace.*
*There is no ignorance, there is knowledge.*
*There is no passion, there is serenity.*
*There is no chaos, there is harmony.*
*There is no death, there is the Force.*

**Restrictions:** No attachments, no possessions, no revenge, serve the Order.

### Sith Code

*Peace is a lie, there is only passion.*
*Through passion, I gain strength.*
*Through strength, I gain power.*
*Through power, I gain victory.*
*Through victory, my chains are broken.*

**The Rule of Two:** One master, one apprentice. Apprentice MUST kill master to advance.

## ROLES

### Smuggler
Fast-talking scoundrel moving cargo others won't touch.
- **Stats:** +2 CHA, +1 DEX
- **Bonus:** 2 underworld contacts, +2 to bluff
- **Special:** SILVER TONGUE — Reroll failed CHA check 1/scene

### Bounty Hunter
Professional tracker bringing in targets.
- **Stats:** +2 DEX, +1 WIS
- **Bonus:** Tracking gear, +2 intimidation
- **Special:** MARK TARGET — +2 all rolls vs designated target until captured

### Soldier
Trained combatant — Republic, Imperial, or mercenary.
- **Stats:** +2 STR or DEX (choose), +1 CON
- **Bonus:** Military weapon, armor, +2 tactics
- **Special:** COMBAT VETERAN — ADV on initiative, give ally ADV on attack (reaction)

### Pilot
Ace flyer at home in any cockpit.
- **Stats:** +2 DEX, +1 INT
- **Bonus:** Ship access OR +3 piloting
- **Special:** EVASIVE — Force reroll on attack vs your vehicle 1/combat

### Tech/Slicer
Engineer, hacker, droid specialist.
- **Stats:** +2 INT, +1 DEX
- **Bonus:** Tools, slicing gear, +2 hacking
- **Special:** JURY-RIG — Temporarily fix broken system 1/scene (lasts 1 hour)

### Scoundrel
Criminal generalist — thief, con artist, dealer.
- **Stats:** +1 DEX, +1 CHA, +1 any
- **Bonus:** Concealed weapon, fake credentials
- **Special:** SLIPPERY — Auto-escape restraints/capture 1/day

### Force User
Force-sensitive on their own path.
- **Stats:** +2 WIS, +1 (INT for Jedi, CHA for Sith, CON for unaligned)
- **Bonus:** Training weapon, Force sensitivity
- **Special:** THE FORCE — Access to Force powers (see FORCE section)

### Diplomat
Political operator with resources and connections.
- **Stats:** +2 CHA, +1 INT
- **Bonus:** 3 political contacts, comfortable wealth
- **Special:** DIPLOMATIC IMMUNITY — Avoid arrest 1/session (not Empire)

## THE FORCE

### Alignment Scale

**-10 (Dark) ←→ 0 (Unaligned) ←→ +10 (Light)**

| Range | Status | Effect |
|-------|--------|--------|
| +7 to +10 | Light Paragon | Light powers -2 DC, Dark powers +4 DC |
| +3 to +6 | Light Side | Jedi norm |
| -2 to +2 | Unaligned | All powers normal |
| -3 to -6 | Dark Side | Sith norm |
| -7 to -10 | Dark Consumed | Dark powers -2 DC, yellow eyes, paranoia |

### Alignment Shifts

**Toward Dark (-):**
- Use Dark power: -1
- Kill in anger: -1
- Torture/cruelty: -1
- Betray for power: -1
- Kill innocent: -2

**Toward Light (+):**
- Spare enemy: +1
- Self-sacrifice: +1
- Refuse Dark temptation: +1
- Redeem Dark user: +2

### Force Strain

Using the Force is taxing. Track Strain to prevent unlimited power spam.

**Max Strain:** WIS + CON (typically 5-10)
**Cost:** Basic = 1 | Standard = 2 | Advanced = 3 | Master = 4
**Recovery:** 1 Strain per hour, OR all on long rest
**Exceed Max:** CON save DC 12 + excess or gain exhaustion level

*Universal powers (Push, Pull, Sense, Jump) at Basic tier cost 0 Strain — the Force flows naturally.*

### Force Powers

**Cast:** d20 + WIS + Force Sensitivity vs DC
**Cost:** Strain equal to power tier (see above)

| Tier | DC | Examples |
|------|-----|----------|
| Basic | 10 | Push (weak), Sense, Jump |
| Standard | 14 | Push (strong), Pull, Mind Trick |
| Advanced | 18 | Choke, Lightning, Heal |
| Master | 22 | Sever Force, Storm |

### Universal Powers

**PUSH:** DC 10 stagger, DC 14 knockdown, DC 18 throw 10m + d6 damage
**PULL:** DC 10 small object, DC 14 disarm, DC 18 pull person
**SENSE:** DC 10 presence 30m, DC 14 emotions/lies, DC 18 through walls
**JUMP/SPEED:** DC 10 double jump, DC 14 triple + extra move, DC 18 superhuman

### Light Side Powers

**MIND TRICK (DC 14):** Suggest action to weak-minded. +2 DC per target WIS above 3.
**HEAL (DC 14-18):** d6 HP, stabilize. DC 18: 2d6, remove condition.
**PROTECTION (DC 16):** +2 AC for 1 minute. Requires Light +1 or higher.

### Dark Side Powers

**CHOKE (DC 16):** Contested WIS. d4/round, disadvantage. Using: -1 alignment.
**LIGHTNING (DC 18):** 2d8 damage, DEX half. Using: -1 alignment.
**DRAIN (DC 20):** Touch, steal d8 HP. Using: -2 alignment.

### Dark Side Temptation

When desperate (below 25% HP, ally dying, overwhelming odds): WIS save.

| Situation | DC |
|-----------|-----|
| Combat danger | 12 |
| Ally threatened | 14 |
| Ally killed | 16 |
| Love interest threatened | 20 |

**Fail:** GM offers Devil's Bargain — power for -1 alignment.
**Crit.Fail:** Use Dark power automatically, -2 alignment.
**Success:** Resist AND +1 alignment (immediate reward for struggle).
**Aid:** Light-aligned ally in scene grants ADV on save. Anchor reduces DC by 2.

### Redemption

Harder than falling. +1 max per session through good deeds.
- Save enemy who tried to kill you: +2
- Sacrifice something precious: +2
- Physical corruption (yellow eyes) fades at -2, gone at +2

### Non-Force User Balance

**Why play without Force?**
- Anonymity (no Force signature to detect)
- Freedom (no Code, no expectations)
- Resources (no poverty vows)
- Specialization (+2 to piloting OR tech/slicing)
- GRIT: 2 points/session — reroll any d20 OR avoid hit that would drop to 0 HP OR auto-succeed skill check in specialty
- Mundane Mastery: +2 to TWO skills of choice (not limited to piloting/tech)

### Lightsaber Forms

Choose one Form. Additional forms require training arc.

| Form | Name | Bonus | Weakness |
|------|------|-------|----------|
| I | Shii-Cho | +1 vs multiple enemies | -1 vs single duelist |
| II | Makashi | +2 vs lightsaber duel | -2 vs blasters |
| III | Soresu | +3 AC, deflect bonus | -1 attack |

### Force Bonds

**Master-Apprentice:** Sense each other across distance. +1 fighting together. If one dies: WIS DC 16 or stunned.
**Life Debt/Attachment:** +2 resist Dark temptation involving them. If harmed: -2 resist Dark.
**Anchor:** Non-Force touchstone (ship, droid, memento). +1 resist temptation near it.

### Shields & Systems

**Shields:** Separate HP pool (ships with shields have this listed).
- Regenerate 5 HP/round if no damage taken that round
- At 0 shields: all damage goes to Hull

**Repair Actions (Tech/Slicer):**
- INT DC 14: Restore d6 shields OR restart one damaged system
- Requires steady course (no evasive maneuvers that round)

**Ion Weapons vs Ships:**
- 10 ion damage = 1 system disabled (roll System Damage)
- 20 ion damage = ship dead in space (all systems offline)

**Boarding:**
- Requires: docking, immobilized target, or breach pod
- Forced entry: STR DC 16 (airlock) or explosives
- Internal combat uses personal scale

### System Damage (on Crit)

| d6 | System | Effect |
|----|--------|--------|
| 1 | Weapons | -1 damage die |
| 2 | Shields | Shields offline |
| 3 | Engines | Speed drops one tier |
| 4 | Hyperdrive | Cannot jump |
| 5 | Sensors | DIS on attacks |
| 6 | Hull Breach | Vacuum exposure |

### Chase Mechanics

**Pursuit Points:** Start 3. At 0 = caught. At 6 = escaped.
Each round: d20 + DEX + Vehicle Handling vs opponent.
- Crit.Fail: Crash, -2 points, 2d6 damage
- Fail: -1 point
- Partial: Stalemate
- Success: +1 point
- Crit: +2 points + free action

## GM TOOLS

### Session Structure: The Job

1. **Acquisition:** Contact offers job. Negotiate (CHA vs contractor).
2. **Travel:** Plot route. Encounter roll on 1-2 (d6).
3. **Execution:** Reach destination, complete objective.
4. **Escape:** If needed, chase rules apply.
5. **Settlement:** Collect pay, pay debts, downtime.

### Escalation Pattern

```
PHASE 1: Negotiation window (CHA)
PHASE 2: Skirmish (2-4 enemies)
PHASE 3: Reinforcements (double)
PHASE 4: Heavy response (vehicles, elites)
PHASE 5: Overwhelming (escape only)
```

### Quick Reference

**Cover:** Light +2 AC | Heavy +4 AC
**Flanking:** 2+ allies in melee = +2 attack each
**High Ground:** +1 ranged attacks
**Suppression:** Forgo attack, pin enemies in area (WIS DC 12)

### The Hunt (Bounty Session)

1. **Briefing:** Receive puck, negotiate "alive" bonus
2. **Research:** Interview informants (CHA), slice networks (INT), stake out (WIS). Each success = +1 final tracking.
3. **Tracking:** Follow trail, false leads on fails
4. **Confrontation:** Ambush, direct ("warm or cold"), deception, siege
5. **Delivery:** Transport (alive targets can escape/bribe), collect payment

### The Mission (Rebel Session)

1. **Briefing:** Command provides objective, incomplete intel
2. **Insertion:** Approach (stealth/deception/assault), entry challenges
3. **Objective:** Sabotage, extraction, elimination, recon
4. **Extraction:** Return to pickup, complications based on escalation
5. **Debrief:** Consequences revealed, reputation adjusted

### Sabacc (Quick Rules)

**Stake:** Agree on credits.
**Play:** d20 + CHA. Highest wins pot.
**Cheating:** DEX DC 14 to cheat (+4 to roll). Fail = caught.
**Caught:** -1 reputation, possible fight, banned from establishment.
**Sabacc Pot:** On tie, pot doubles. Pure Sabacc (natural 20) = win pot + 2× stake.
